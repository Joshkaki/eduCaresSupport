How can I set up student attendance type for an institution?

Based on how student attendance is marked in your institution, attendance can be set to any one of the following options:

  • Daily – Student attendance must be marked once daily.
  • Subject-wise – Student attendance must be marked for each class/period.
  1. Click the module access icon > Administration > Settings > General settings to open the General settings page.
  2. Select the student attendance type, and then click the Update button.
