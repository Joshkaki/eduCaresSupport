How can I mark student attendance daily?

By default all students are considered present in EduCares. You will only need to mark attendance for students who are absent. Students can be marked absent only for the current date or a past date, students cannot be marked absent for a future date.

  1. Click the module access icon > Academics > Attendance > Attendance register to open the Attendance registerpage.
  2. Select the batch. All students of the selected batch are displayed in the timetable. The radio buttons Name and Roll number will allow you to sort students by name or roll number.

    The current date is highlighted in yellow, and the days set as ‘days off’ are greyed out. Use the arrows at the top of the timetable to view the attendance for a previous month.
  3. Select the date against the student you want to mark absent.
  4. Enter the reason for the student being absent.
  5. Select the session the student was absent for.
  6. Click the Addbutton. Days marked absent are indicated by an ‘A’
