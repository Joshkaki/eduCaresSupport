About Messages

Message section in EduCares is used for internal messaging. With Messages the administrator, employees, students, and parents can send and receive private and secure messages to other EduCares users based on their role. Messages also function as a notification system by sending notifications to EduCares users who are part of or involved in certain EduCares events.

Who can send messages?

User Recipients
Administrator Can send messages to all EduCares users.
Employee – Teacher/Tutor Can send messages to:
• All Employees
• All Students
• Only parents of the students who they teach.
Employee – Staff (Not a teacher or tutor) Can send messages to:
• All Employees
• All Students
Student Can send messages to:
• Teachers/tutors of only their batch
• Students of only their batch
• Only their guardian
Parent Can send messages to:
• Only teachers/tutors who teach their children
• Only their children
