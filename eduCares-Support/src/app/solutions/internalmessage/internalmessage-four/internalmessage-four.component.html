How can I delete a message?

You can delete individual messages immediately after reading them or from the Inbox page.

  1. . At the top of your dashboard, click the message icon to open the Inbox page.
  2. Click the message you want to read.
  3. On the top right, click the Delete Conversation button, and then click OK to confirm.
