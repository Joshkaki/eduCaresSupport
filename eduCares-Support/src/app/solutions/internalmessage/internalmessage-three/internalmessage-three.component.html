How can I reply to a message?

When a message is received, the message icon at the top of your dashboard will notify you that a new message was received.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the message icon to open the Inbox page.
  2. Click the message you want to read.
  3. In the Subject field, enter the subject of the message.
  4. In the Message field, enter the message.
  5. Click the Send button.
