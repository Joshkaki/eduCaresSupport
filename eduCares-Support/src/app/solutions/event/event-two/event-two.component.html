How to edit an event?

Only holiday events can be edited from the Calendar. General events cannot be edited. Other events such as examinations and book/fee dues must be edited from their respective modules.

To edit a holiday event:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Calendar to open the Calendar.
  2. Hover over the green icon that indicates a Holiday. The event details are displayed on the right
  3. Click Edit beside the event details.
  4. Make the changes and click the Update event button.
