How to delete an event?

Only general events and holidays can be deleted from the Calendar. Other events such as examinations and book/fee dues must be deleted from their respective modules.

To delete a general event:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Calendar to open the Calendar.
  2. However over the black icon that indicates a general event. The event details are displayed on the right.
  3. Click Delete beside the event details to delete the event.

To delete a holiday event:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Calendar to open the Calendar.
  2. Hover over the green icon that indicates a Holiday. The event details are displayed on the right.
  3. Click Delete beside the event details to delete the event.
