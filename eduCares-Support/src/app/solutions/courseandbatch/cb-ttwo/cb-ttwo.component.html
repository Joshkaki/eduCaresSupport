Can we delete all students in a batch at a time?

In EduCares, we cannot delete all students of Batch/Course/Institution at a time. We can perform batch transfer or can graduate students in bulk, but we cannot delete in bulk. To perform deletion you have to go to their respective individual profile and click on delete.

When archiving or deleting a student account, the following two options are available:

  • Student leaving institution – Use this option to archive and move the student from the active student list to the archived/former student list.
  • Remove student records – Use this option to delete all information associated with the student. Once deleted, the student account cannot be restored.So it should be decided with double confirmation.
