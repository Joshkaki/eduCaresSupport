How to Collect Instant Fee?

To collect an instant fee, the Admin/Privilege user needs to perform the below steps:

Step 1: Go to Menu > Administration > Finance > Fee > Instant Fee

Step 2: Click on the Pay fees option.

Step 3: Click on the Radio button to choose the type of the Payee
Step 4: Enter the Name of the Payee.
Step 5: Click on the Name of the Payee.

Step 6: Select the category of the fee which needs to be collected from the drop-down list.

Step 7: Click on the Collect option to collect the fees.
