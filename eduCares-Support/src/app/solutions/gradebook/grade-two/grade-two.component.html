What is gradebook?

Gradebook also referred to as report card or mark sheet or scorecard is any system used to record a studentâ€™s progress during the academic year via marks or grades.

Gradebook module is used in Educares to manage the grading profiles used in school, to schedule exams, to enter marks and to generate report cards for the school.

To access gradebook - Menu >> Academics >> Gradebook

The main steps involved in Gradebook setup are as follows-

  • Do Gradebook setting
  • Create Exam Planners
  • Manage Gradebook
  • Gradebook reports
