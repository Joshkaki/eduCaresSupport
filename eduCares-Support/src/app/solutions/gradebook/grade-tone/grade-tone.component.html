How to publish marks to parents?

Once mark entry is completed and the grade book report is generated, marks can be published to parents. Only after that parents or students will be able to view the marks in Educares.

To publish mark, choose the Publish Report button after the report generation is completed.

To send SMS Notification to parents, do select send Notification.


Once the report is successfully published the report Status will get updated as Published.
