How can I edit the leave balance of an employee?

Only an administrator or a privileged employee (Employee Attendance privilege is required) can edit the leave balance for an employee.

  1. Go to the user’s profile.
  2. In the top right, click Leaves > My Leaves. The employee’s leave details are displayed.
  3. In the top right, click the Employee leave balance button to open the Employee Leave Balance page as depicted in Fig 1. For each leave type, the available leave credit is displayed.
  4. Click Edit beside a leave type.
  5. Enter the number of leaves to be added, and then click the Save button.
