How to check leave request status?

The employee can keep track of the leaves taken and the status of leave from the My Leaves page.

  1. Go to your profile. In the top right, click Leaves > My leaves to open the My Leaves page.
    Or
    At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Leaves > My Leaves to open the My Leaves page.
  2. In the top right, click the My leave applications button. All your leave applications that were submitted after the last ‘Leave Reset’ date will be displayed along with its status (Pending, Approved, or Denied). You can filter your leave applications based on status.

Use the date filter to view leave applications that were submitted before the last ‘Leave Reset’ date. Click View beside a leave application to view its details.
