How can I generate the employees additional leaves report?

The employee additional leaves report will display the number of additional leaves taken by an employee even after exhausting all leaves.

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Human Resource > Employee Leave Management > Attendance Report > Additional Leaves button.

  1. Use the month, year, and department filters, and then click the View button.
  2. Click the CSV Report button to generate a PDF report.
