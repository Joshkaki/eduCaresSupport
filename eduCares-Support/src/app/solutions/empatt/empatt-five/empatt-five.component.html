How can I reverse leave request for an employee?

As a manager, you can view all leave requests for an employee and reverse an approved or denied leave request if necessary.

  1. At the top of the dashboard, click the module access icon> Academics > Leaves > Reportee’s Leaves to open the Reportee’s Leaves page.All employees who report to you are listed along with their leave summary details.
  2. Select the employee for whom you want to revert a leave application. All leave applications for the employee are displayed.
  3. Click on the leave application you want to reverse
  4. Enter any remarks as depicted in Fig 1.
  5. Click the Deny button to deny the leave application.
