How can an employee apply for a leave?

Leave requests are sent to your manager who has the option to approve or deny your leave. Employees can apply for leave from the Leaves module or from your employee profile.

  1. Go to your profile. In the top right, click Leaves > Apply leave to open the Leave Application page. Or
  2. If an employee is a manager - At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Leaves > Apply leave button to open the Leave Application page.
    Or
  3. If a user is not a manager - At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Apply leave to open the Leave Application page.
  4. Select the leave duration as Single day, Multiple days, or Half day.
  5. Select the leave date or date range. Note: you cannot select a date or date range for which a leave application already exists
  6. In the Leave type field, select the leave type. Note: You will be notified if it is an additional leave, and if it is liable for salary deduction.
  7. In the Reason field, enter the reason for applying the leave.
  8. Click the Submit button.
