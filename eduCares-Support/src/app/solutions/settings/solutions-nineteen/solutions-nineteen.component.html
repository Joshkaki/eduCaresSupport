Custom Word Settings In EduCares

Custom Words are used to manage the variations in terms of names of batches, sections or courses, etc. in the Institution inside the EduCares. Admin or the privileged user can add the custom words which helps to display the required words in the EduCares UI.

In order to create an Academic Year in EduCares System, Admin or the privileged user has to follow the below steps:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the Menu > Administration > Settings > Academic Years to open the Custom words page.
  2. Click on the radio button to enable and enter the custom words.
  3. Enter the custom words for a different entity which are:
    1. Course: Course can be called as Class in the institute.
    2. Batch: Batch can be called as Section in the institute.
    3. Subject: Subject can be called Paper etc.
      The user needs to enter the Lower Case and Lower Case Plural for creating the custom words.
  4. Click on the Save changes button to save the configuration.
