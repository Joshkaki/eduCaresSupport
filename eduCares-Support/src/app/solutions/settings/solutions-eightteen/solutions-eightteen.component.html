Configure Academic Year

Academic Year is the year range for the institution's academic-related activities like exams, timetable, etc. You might name it as 2019-2020, 2020-2021. All the students getting admitted, exams created and scheduled, sections created will be under an academic year that helps to organize the events and data chronologically for better efficiency.

In order to create an Academic Year in EduCares System, Admin or the privileged user has to follow the below steps:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the Menu > Administration > Settings > Academic Years to open the manage Academic Year page.
  2. Click on the New Academic Year button to create a new academic year.
  3. Enter the details of Start Date, End Date, and Academic Year Name to create the academic year.
  4. Click on Create Academic Year to save the configuration.

Note:

If the user wants to mark the academic year which has been created as Active Academic Year, then the below steps need to be completed.

  1. Click on the Change Active Academic Year button.
  2. Select the Academic Year from the drop-down menu.
  3. Click on Save Active Year to save the settings
