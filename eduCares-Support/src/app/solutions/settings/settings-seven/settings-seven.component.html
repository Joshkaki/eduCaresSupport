How to assign an elective subject to a student?

The administrator and privileged employees (Manage Course/Batch or Subject master privilege must be is provided) can assign an elective subject to a student through the Manage Course page, the Manage Batch page, or the Subjects page.

Students will also have the option to select an elective subject only if you have selected the Enable elective selection checkbox when creating the course

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Settings > Manage Course/Batch > Manage Batch to open the Manage Batch page.

Or

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Settings > Manage Course/Batch > Manage Course to open the Manage Course page.

Or

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Settings > Manage Course/Batch > Manage Subject to open the Subjects page.

1. Select the Course. All batches of the selected course are displayed.

2. Select the batch.

3. In the top right, click Manage > Subject to display Elective Groups. Existing elective groups will be displayed.

4. Click Assign Student beside the elective subject you want to assign as depicted in Figure.

5. Click Assign beside the student you want to assign.
