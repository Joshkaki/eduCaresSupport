How can I view the timetable for a batch?

Timetables created for a batch/section can be viewed and printed as a PDF or CSV file. In a printed timetable, breaks are indicated with the duration of the break scheduled.

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon >Academics > Timetable > View Timetables to open the View Timetable page. Select the timetable range, and then select a batch and view the timetable.

Click the PDF report button to print the timetable as a PDF file, or click the CSV report button to print the timetable as a CSV file.
