How can I add additional rooms to a building in timetable?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Timetable > Class Room Allocation > Manage Buildings to open the Manage Buildings page.
  2. Select the building for which you want to add more rooms.
  3. In the top right, click the Add Rooms button.
  4. In the Room Name field, enter the room name.
  5. In the Capacity field, enter the number of students the room can accommodate.
  6. Click the Save button.
