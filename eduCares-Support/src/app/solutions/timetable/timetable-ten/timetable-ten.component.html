How can teachers view their timetable?

Teachers can view their timetables from their user profile only if they are assigned to a subject and a timetable is created for that subject. They need to login to their respective accounts. They will receive a notification of the scheduled timetable.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > My Profile to open your profile.
  2. In the top right, click the Timetable button.
  3. Select the timetable you want to view.
  4. Click on the Activities button at the top right corner to view the timetable for a specific day or, can access it from Employee Profile > More > Activities.
  5. Click the PDF report button to print the timetable as a PDF file.
  6. Click the CSV report button to print the timetable as a CSV file.
