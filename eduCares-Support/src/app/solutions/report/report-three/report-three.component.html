How can I generate reports of employees in Educares?

The administrator or a privileged employee (View Additional Reports privilege is required) can view the list of active employees from the Reports center.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Reports to open the Report center.
  2. Click on Employee which will list all the active employees along with their joining date, department, employee position, current manager, and gender.
    Click on the name of a employee to view the employee's profile.
  3. Click the Export as CSV button to export the details as a CSV file.
  4. When the file status changes from In Queue to Download , click Download to download and save the file to your system. This step may take a while.

Note: Make sure to keep refreshing the web page.
