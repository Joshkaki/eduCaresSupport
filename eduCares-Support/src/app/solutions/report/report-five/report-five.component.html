How can I generate the list of former employees?

The Former employees Details report lists all the former/archived employees. The administrator or a privileged employee (View Additional Reports privilege is required) can access this report from the Reports center.

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Reports > Former employees Details to open the Reports center.

The report lists all archived employees during the specified date range. The report includes each employeeâ€™s joining date, leaving date, department, employee position, manager, and gender.

  1. Click on the name of a employee to view the archived employee's profile.
  2. Click the Export as CSV button to export the details as a CSV file.
  3. When the file status changes from In Queue to Download, click Download and save the file to your system. This step may take a while.

Note: Make sure to keep refreshing the web page.
