How can I generate exam schedule report?

After creating an exam schedule, the administrator or a privileged employee (View Additional Reports privilege is required) can generate the exam schedule report.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Reports > Exam Schedule Details to open the Exam Schedule Details page.

    Exam schedules for all batches will be displayed here. The report includes the exam type, exam name, the maximum and minimum marks, and also the start time and end time of each exam.

  2. To view the exam schedule for a particular batch, select a course, and then select a batch.
  3. Click the Search button.
  4. Click the Export as CSV button to export the exam schedule.

Note: Make sure to keep refreshing the web page.
