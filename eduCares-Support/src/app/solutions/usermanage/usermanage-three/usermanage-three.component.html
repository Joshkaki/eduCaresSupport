How can we assign privileges to an employee?

Employee privileges are usually set during admission and can be edited by the administrator or a privileged employee (Manage Users privilege is required) at any time. Student and Parent privileges cannot be edited as they are pre-set.

Note: Only admin or privileged employee(Manage users privilege) can change privilege for an employee.

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > User to open the Manage Users page.

  1. Search for the user
  2. When you find the user, click on the user’s name. Basic details of the user’s profile is displayed.
  3. Click the Edit Privilege button. The Edit Privilege page is displayed.
