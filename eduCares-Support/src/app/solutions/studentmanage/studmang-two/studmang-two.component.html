How can I view a student profile and reports?

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Students > Student details to open the Student details page.

  1. Select Present students to search for active students or select Former students to search for archived/former students.
  2. In the Searchfield, enter the student’s first name, last name, or admission number to search.

Note: You must enter at least the first three letters a of the student’s first or last name. All students whose name begins with those letters will be displayed.

On click on the name of the student it will take to 'Student Profile' page as shown in the screenshot.

You can see a link called 'Report' if you click on the link it will take you to report page of the student as shown in the screenshot.
