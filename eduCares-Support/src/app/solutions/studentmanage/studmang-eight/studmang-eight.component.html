How to view the list of the archived students?

The Former Students Details report lists all former/archived students. The administrator or a privileged employee (View Additional Reports privilege is required) can access this report from the Reports center.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Reports > Former Students Details to open the Reports center.
    The report lists all the students who were archived during the specified date range. The report includes each student’s admission date, leaving date, batch, course, and gender.

    Click on the name of a student to view the archived student’s profile.

  2. Click the Export as CSV button to export the details as a CSV file.
  3. When the file status changes from In Queue to Download, click Download to download and save the file to your system. This step may take a while.

Note: Make sure to keep refreshing the web page.
