Can I create instant fee particulars or fee discounts at the time of collecting fees?

Yes, when collecting fees from a student through the Finance module, you can create an instant fee particular or an instant fee discount. You can collect fees:

  • By course – Module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Collect the Fees > Fee Submission by Course

  • For each student – Module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Collect the Fees > Fee Submission for each student

  • By fee particular – Module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Collect the Fees > Particular wise Fee Payment

    Click Add Particular to add a fee particular, and then enter the name and the amount.

    Click Add Discount to add a fee discount, and then enter the name, the mode, and the discount percentage or amount.
