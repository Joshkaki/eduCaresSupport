How can I set up and edit / delete fines for late fee payments?

You can set up fine slabs that define the amount the students will be charged in case of late fee payments.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Create Fees > Generate Fine.
  2. In the Fine name field, enter the name of the fine.
  3. In the Days after due date field, enter the number of days after the fee collection due date that the corresponding fine amount will be applied.
  4. In the Fine amount field, enter the fine amount that will be applied based on the fine mode.
    If the fine mode is Percentage, the percentage to be paid as a fine is calculated on the student fee amount. If the mode is Amount, the amount entered in the Fine amount field will be the fixed amount that will be applied as fine.
  5. Click the Create button. If you want to add another fine slab, click Add fine slab and enter the details.

Edit/Delete fines for late fee payments:

Fines created for late fee payments can be edited only if you have not started processing student fee payments. However, fines can be deleted at any time. To delete fine first delete all fine slabs, and then click the Update button.
