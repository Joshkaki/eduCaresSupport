About News?

In News module, the administrator and the privileged employees (Manage news privilege is required) can publish timely announcements to keep all Educares users informed.

Every announcement published has a comments section at the end to allow readers to post comments. Comments allow readers to have a discussion with the person posting the news and with others. If required, you can moderate the comments being posted.
