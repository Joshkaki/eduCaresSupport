How can I compare finance transactions between two different time intervals?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Finance Reports > Compare Transactions.
  2. Select the start date and end date of the two financial periods you want to compare.
  3. Click the View Report button.

Click on a finance category to drill down and view individual transaction details.
