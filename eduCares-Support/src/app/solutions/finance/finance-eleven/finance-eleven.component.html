How can I view the donation transactions for a specific period?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Donations to open the Donations page.
  2. Select a date range for which you want to view the transactions.
  3. Click the CSV report button to generate a CSV report with the list of transactions.
