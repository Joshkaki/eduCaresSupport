How can I create an income transaction?

Income transactions can be created when money is received by the institution.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Transactions > Add income to open the Create new income page.
  2. In the Title field, enter the name of the income transaction.
  3. In the Description field, enter the short description about the income transaction.
  4. In the Amount field, enter the income amount.
  5. In the Date field, select the transaction date.
  6. In the Category field, select the finance category that applies to this income transaction.
  7. Click the Save button.
