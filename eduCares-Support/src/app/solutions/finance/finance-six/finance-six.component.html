How can I delete an income transactions?

An Income in transactions can be deleted at any time. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Transactions > Add income to open the Create new income page.

  1. In the top right, click the Incomes button.
  2. Select the start date and end date.
  3. Click the OK button. All income transactions are listed.
  4. Click Delete to delete a transaction.
  5. In the top right, click the PDF report button to generate a PDF report of the income transactions.
