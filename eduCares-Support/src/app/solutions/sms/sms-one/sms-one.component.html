How to configure SMS settings for various scenarios in Educares?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon Collaboration > SMS > SMS Settings to open the Settings page.
  2. Select the Application checkbox, and then click the Update button.
  3. Select the events for which a notification will be sent, and the recipients who will receive the notification.
  4. Click the Update button.
