How to activate or inactivate course for online registration?

A course can be activated for online registration while setting it up or at a later time. When a course is active, applicants can register for the course online. When a course is not active, applicants will not be able to register.

  1. Click the module access icon > Academics > Applicant> Settings > Active Years and Courses.
  2. Select the academic year and click on Edit. All the of the academic year will be displayed.
  3. Click on the Check box to Activate and Inactivate the course
  4. Click on Save.
