How can I view the list of applicants registered for a particular course?

  1. Click the module access icon > Academics > Applicant Registration.
  2. Applicants registered for the course are displayed.
  3. Filter the applicants based on the applicant statuses or a date range.
  4. Click the PDF Report to generate a PDF report with the list of applicants.
  5. Click the CSV Export to generate a CSV report with the list of applicants.
