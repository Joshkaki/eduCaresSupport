How to allot a student applicant to a course?

You can regularly review the applicants registered for a course and accordingly accept or decline their admission.

  1. Click the module access icon > Academics > Applicant Registration.
  2. Click the applicant’s name to review the applicant’s admission form.
  3. To allot the applicant to a course, select the applicant.
  4. Select the batch, and then click the Allot button.
    Note: To decline admission click the Discard button.
  5. Next Click on Update Status Button
  6. Then Mark as Paid under Payment Status Option.
  7. Then Click on Update Status Button.

Then The Student is allocated to the applied course.
Then download the Fee Receipt & Application in PDF format.
