How can I edit or delete sales invoice?

A sales invoice can be edited or deleted only if the customer has not paid for the item, i.e., the Is Paid checkbox was not selected when creating the sales invoice.

  1. Click module access icon > Administration > Inventory > Billing to open the Invoice page.
  2. Select a store. All sales invoices of the store are displayed. If you are searching for a particular invoice, select the store and enter the invoice number to search.
  3. Click Edit to edit the sales invoice or click Delete to delete the sales invoice.
