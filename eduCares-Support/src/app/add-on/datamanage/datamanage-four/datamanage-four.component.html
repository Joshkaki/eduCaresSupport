How can I add data or values in Data Management category?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Data Management
  2. Click on the category name from the category list.
  3. In the page opens click on New Entry at the right top.
  4. Update the respective field provided.
  5. Click on Save.
