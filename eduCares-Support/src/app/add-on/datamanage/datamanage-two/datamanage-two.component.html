How can I create category and fields in it?

Only Admin or Privileged employee can perform data management in EduCares

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Data Management> New
  2. Provide the category name, update the field name and corresponding input method.
  3. Additional field can be provided by clicking on Add field option.
  4. Click on Save.
  5. Once category and fields are created it will be displayed in Data management home page.
