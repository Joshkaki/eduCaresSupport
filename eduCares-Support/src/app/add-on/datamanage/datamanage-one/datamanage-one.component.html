Data Management Plugin

Data management module deals with adding categories of different files,equipments etc in an Institution. It is similiar to that of Inventory in EduCares, only difference is that it doesn't sell or issue items. It only tracks the items or equipments such as computer, printers, furniture such as desk, chair etc in an Institution.
