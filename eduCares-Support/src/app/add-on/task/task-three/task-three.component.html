How 'Filter by ' option is helpful to view tasks?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Collaboration > Tasks.
  2. In the page opens, Filter option helps to filter or list the task as All, Assigned, Completed, Due Date under the two categories: Task assigned to me and Task assigned by me.
