How to upload picture in the album?

Once the Album is created, the user needs to follow the below steps to add or upload pictures in the album.

Steps:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Collaboration > Gallery
  2. 2. Select the album in which you want to add the picture by clicking on it.
  3. 3. Click on the Add Images button.
  4. 4. Click on the Select Images to upload button to upload the image from the computer. Note: You can also drag and drop the image from the file that is stored on the computer.
  5. 5. You can upload multiple images by using the Select More Images option.
  6. 6.Click on Upload Images to the Album button to upload the image.
