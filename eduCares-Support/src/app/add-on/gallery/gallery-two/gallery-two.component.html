How can I edit category/album name in gallery?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Collaboration > Gallery
  2. Click on the Album you want to edit.
  3. Go for the 'Edit' option on the right-top as shown in the Fig. which lets you to edit the album name, visibility of album.
