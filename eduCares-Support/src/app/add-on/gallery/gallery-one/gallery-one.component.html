About Gallery Module

Gallery module is used to post pictures of events happened in the institution. The administrator and privileged employees can post the pictures of latest events like annual day, seminar etc that happened in the institution and can select the employees, students from departments and batches. Those Students and employees can view picture which are shared with them.
