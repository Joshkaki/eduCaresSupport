How to view the result of a Poll?

Members can view the result or status of an active poll by clicking on title of the poll we can see the results if and only if they have updated their opinion on the corresponding poll.

Login as student or employee.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Colloboration> Poll.
  2. Click on the respective poll.
