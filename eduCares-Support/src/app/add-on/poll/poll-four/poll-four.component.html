How can a user (student/employee) give their respective votes?

For voting, log in as a student or employee.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Colloboration> Poll.
  2. Click on the respective poll.
  3. Select an option and click Vote.
  4. Then click on poll in the online poll.
