Can a user reopen a closed poll?

Yes, their is a option for open.

Admin or privileged user(member of that poll) has the option to reopen a poll.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Colloboration> Poll.
  2. Click on Open to reopen the respective poll, which is unpublished under Closed.
