How can I create polls in EduCares?

Only Admin or Privileged employee can create poll in EduCares.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Colloboration> Poll> New Poll
  2. Provide the Title, Descriptions, update the Answers (option to poll) and select members.
  3. Additional field can be provided by clicking on Add another option.
  4. Click on Post poll.
  5. Once category and fields are created it will be displayed in Poll home page.
