About Online Payment

The Online Payment plugin will allow you set up payment gateways, so users make online payments for registration, for any type of fees, etc. Online payments can be enabled for:

  • Student fee (Fees schedules created in the Finance module)
  • Application registration fee
  • Hostel fee
  • Transport fee

Educares supports the following payment gateways:

  • PayPal
  • Interswitch
  • Authorize.net.
  • Stripe
  • Braintree
  • PayU
  • CCavenue

In addition to the payment gateways that Educares will support, institutions can also integrate any custom payment gateway with Educares. This will offer institutions greater control and flexibility for online payments.
