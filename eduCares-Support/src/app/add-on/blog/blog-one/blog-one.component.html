How a student or employee can create a blog?

In EduCares only students and employees can participate in blogging. Admin cannot participate in blogging.

  • At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Collaboration> blog> My blog> New Post.
  • Provide the Title and Body.
  • Click on Post to publish poll or save as draft to publish it later.
