How can I view the custom import logs?

Maintaining a log of the files imported into eduCares is always useful. The All Exports page will let you know when a custom import was performed and whether the records were properly imported or not.

  • At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Custom Imports to open the All Exports page.
  • Click Imports.
