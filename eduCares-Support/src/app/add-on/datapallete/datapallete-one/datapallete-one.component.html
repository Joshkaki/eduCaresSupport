About Data Palette

The EduCares institution dashboard is a collection of dashlets that give you an overview of important information for a specific date, generated from its complementary page. The dashlets let you monitor the progress of your institution. You can easily rearrange the dashlets using drag-and-drop, and you can also choose the dashlets you want to display on the dashboard.
