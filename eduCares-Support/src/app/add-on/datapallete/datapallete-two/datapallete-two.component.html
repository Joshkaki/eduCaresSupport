How do I add a dashlet to my dashboard?

To add a dashlet to your dashboard:

  1. On your dashboard, click the Manage Dashlets button.
  2. All available dashlets are displayed.
  3. Select the dashlets to be displayed on your dashboard, and then click the Save button.
