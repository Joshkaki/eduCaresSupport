How can I edit or delete a book?

You can edit or delete book details only if the book has not been issued to a student or employee. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics > Library > Manage Books to open the Manage Books page.

  1. Click Edit beside the book you want to edit. You can edit the book details and update the same.
  2. Click Delete beside the book you want to delete.
