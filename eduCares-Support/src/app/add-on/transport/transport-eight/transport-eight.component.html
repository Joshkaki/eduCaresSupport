How to add new stops to an existing route?

  1. At the top of the dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Transport > Routes to open the Route Details page.
  2. In the Route Details page, click the Edit link against a route for which you wish to add new stop details.
  3. Click on +Add to assign new stops along a particular route.
  4. Enter the stop details.
  5. Click on Save button.
