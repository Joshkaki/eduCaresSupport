How can I change the 'stop' point of a user?

At the top of your dashboard, click on Menu > Administration> Transport > Assign Transport.

  1. Select the Course and Batch and click on the student/employee name
  2. Enter the desired stop (destination) point in the respective fields as shown in the above figure.
  3. Click on Assign.
