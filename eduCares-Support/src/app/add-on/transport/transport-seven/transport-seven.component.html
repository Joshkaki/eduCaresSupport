How to set up a route?

Routes can be created once all the stops are added. When entering route details, custom fields can be added prior to record any additional information required by an institution (Administration > Transport > Settings > Route Additional Details).

  1. At the top of the dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Transport > Routes to open the Route Details page.
  2. In the top right, click the Add Route button to open the Add New Route page.
  3. In the Name field, enter the name of the route.
  4. In the Flat route fare field, enter the fee to be paid.
  5. Select the Vehicle, Driver and Attendant for that route in Vehicle, Driver and Attendant field respectively.
  6. Fill the additional route detail fields.
  7. Assign the stops with Pickup Time, Drop Time, Distance and fare.
  8. Click the Save button.
