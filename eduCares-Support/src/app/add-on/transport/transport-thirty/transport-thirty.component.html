How can I assign Drivers and Attendants?

At the top of the dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Transport > Drivers and Attendants to manage Driver and Attendant Details.

Step 1: Click on 'Add Employees'.

Step 2: Select the department from the list.

Step 3: Select the employee and change 'Job' field to Driver or Attendant as shown below and 'Save'.
