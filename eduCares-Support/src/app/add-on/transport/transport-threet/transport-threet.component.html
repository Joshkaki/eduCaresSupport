How can I assign student or employee to a Route?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Transport > Assign Transport.
  2. To search for a student, select the Student option and then enter the course and batch of the student to search. To search for an employee, select the Employee option and then enter the department of the employee to search.
  3. Click on the label and fill the details about the pickup and drop.
  4. Select the Auto Update Fare if you want to update the fare to the existing fare.
  5. Click Assign to assign the student/employee to a vehicle.
    Note:
    • Students can be assigned to the vehicle even if seats are not available.
    • Bulk information about the passengers can be imported using csv files.
