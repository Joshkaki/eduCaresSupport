How new vehicle details can be added?

  1. At the top of the dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Transport > Settings > Vehicle to open theVehicle details page.
  2. In the top right, click the Add Vehicle button.
  3. In the Vehicle Number field, enter the registration number of the vehicle.
  4. In the Type field, enter the type of vehicle: Own, Leased or Vendor.
  5. In the Vehicle Model field, enter the model of the vehicle.
  6. In the GPS number field, enter the GPS number allocated to the vehicle.
  7. In the No. of seats button, enter the total number of seats available in the vehicle.
  8. Specify the status of the vehicle:
    • Active – In service.
    • Inactive– Not in service.
  9. Enter the details in the additional fields provided.
  10. Click the Save button.
