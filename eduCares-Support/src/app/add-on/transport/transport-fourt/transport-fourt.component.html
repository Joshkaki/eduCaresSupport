How can I change the route details for a student or employee?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click on Menu > Administration > Transport > Assign Transport
  2. To search for a student, select the Student option and then select the course and batch of the student to search as shown in Figure (1). To search for an employee, select the Employee option and then select the department of the employee to search as shown in Figure (2)
    Figure (1) Student search

    Figure (2) Employee search
  3. Click Remove to unassign the student/employee from the vehicle.
  4. Click on the labels available of the list of employees/students, make the changes and click on Assign.
