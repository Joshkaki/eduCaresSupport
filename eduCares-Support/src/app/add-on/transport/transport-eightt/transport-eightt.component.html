How to unassign a transport fee collection for a student or employee?

At the top of your dashboard, click on Menu > Administration > Transport > Transport Fee > Fee Collections > Manage Fee Collection to open the Manage Fee Collection page.

  1. In the top right, click the Manage Fee Collections button to open the Manage Fee Collection page.
  2. Select the Student or Employee option.
  3. If you have selected the Student option, you must select a batch. All students and their corresponding transport fee collections are listed. You can then unassign the required fee collection(s).

    OR

    If you have selected the Employee option, you must select an employee department. All employees and their corresponding transport fee collections are listed. You can then unassign the required fee collection(s).
  4. Click the Update button.
