How to add vehicle certificate type?

Create your vehicle related certificates type here by following the below steps
Step 1: Transport -> Settings -> Vehicle Certificate type -> Add details

Step 2: Attach the certificate type after adding the vehicle .Click here to know how to add vehicle details in Educares.

Step 3: Click on Vehicle Certificate -> Upload certificate.

Step 4: Select the certificate type and fill all the details. You can also set reminder here upload the Certificate.
