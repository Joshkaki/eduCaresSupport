How can I assign an existing transport fee collection to a student/employee?

You can assign and unassign the scheduled transport fee collections for a student or employee by following the below steps,

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click on Menu > Administration > Transport > Transport Fee > Fee Collection > Manage Fee Collection to open the Manage Fee Collection page.
  2. Select the Student option and then enter the batch of the student to search.
  3. Click on student name, select the fee collections which are to be assigned to that particular student.
  4. Select the employee option to assign fee collection to employees.
    Note: You can only search for students and employees who have been assigned transport.
  5. Click the Update button.
