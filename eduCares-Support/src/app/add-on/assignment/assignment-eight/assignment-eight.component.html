Who all can able to view assignment module in Educares?

In Educares Admin login doesn't contain assignment plugin. A teacher can view assignment module only when he/she has got associated with a subject. To assign subject to teacher please follow below steps.

  1. Click the module access icon > Administration > Human Resource > Employee Management > Employee Subject Association.
  2. In the Batch field, select the batch.
  3. In the Subject field, select the subject.
  4. In the Department field, select the department. All employees who belong to the selected department are displayed.
  5. Click Assign beside the employee whom you want to assign the subject. Assigned employees will be displayed under the Currently assigned list.
  6. Click Unassign to remove the employee from the assigned subject.

Note: You cannot remove an employee from an assigned subject if the timetable has already been created for the subject.
