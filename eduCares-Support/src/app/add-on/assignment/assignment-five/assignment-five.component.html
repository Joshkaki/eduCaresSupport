How students can submit assignments in Educares?

Students can upload word-processed documents, spreadsheets, images, and even videos. When students log in to Educares, students can see the list of assignments to be completed along with the due dates in the Assignment module.

When students click on the name of an assignment, the full description of the assignment is displayed.

The students can then click the Answer this assignment button and submit their work.

After entering the assignment details and attaching the necessary files, the student can click the Submit Answer button.
