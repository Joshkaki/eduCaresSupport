About Assignment Plugin?

The Assignment plugin is designed to help teachers distribute homework and assignments to students, collect completed work, review it and provide feedback to students. When distributing work, you can attach digital content such as, word-processed documents, spreadsheets, and images. Similarly, you can instruct students to submit their work as an uploaded file, or students can also type directly into a text field in Educares.

Each assignment must be assigned a ‘due date’. Though Educares will allow submission of work even if the due date for an assignment has passed, you can choose whether to accept or reject the assignment.
