How can we view the students who has not paid hostel fees?

If a student has not paid the assigned fees by the fee collection due date, the student will be listed as a fee defaulter.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Hostel fee defaulters to open the View hostel fee defaulters page.
  2. Select a batch and a fee collection. All student fee defaulters are listed.
  3. Process the fee payment for each student by selecting the students in the top left, or by using the arrow icons to navigate to the next student record.
