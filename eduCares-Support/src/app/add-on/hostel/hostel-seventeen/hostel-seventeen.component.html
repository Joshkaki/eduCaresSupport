How to unassign a hostel fee collection for a student?

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Fee Collection > User-wise Fee Collections to open the Fee Collections page.

  1. In the top right, click the Manage Fee Collections button to open the Manage Fee Collection page.
  2. Select a batch. All students and their corresponding hostel fee collections are listed. You can then unassign the required fee collection(s).
  3. Click the Update button.
