How to edit or delete a scheduled fee collection?

Scheduled fee collection dates can be edited at any time. However, if a student has paid the fees, you cannot delete the fee collection.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Fee Collection > View to open the View fee collection dates page.
  2. Select a batch.
  3. Click Edit to edit the fee collection dates or click Delete to delete a fee collection.
