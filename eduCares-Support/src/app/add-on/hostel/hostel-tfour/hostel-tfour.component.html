From where will we get the detailed report on hostel rooms?

The Room details report displays information about each hostel including the hostel type, the number of rooms available, the number of rooms occupied, and the name of the students allotted to a room.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Report to open the Room details report.
  2. Each item in the table can be clicked to view additional hostel details.
  3. Click the Export as CSV button to export the information to a CSV file.
