How can I allocate students to a hostel?

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Room Allocation.
  2. To search for a student, enter the name or admission number of the student to search.
  3. Click on the name of the student.
  4. Select a hostel.
    Note: Based on the gender of the student, the type of hostel will be listed.
  5. Click Allocate beside the room you want to allocate to a student.
