How to change the hostel warden?

At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel to open the Hostel Detailspage.

  1. Click View beside the hostel.
  2. In the top right, click the Manage Wardens button
  3. Click Remove to remove a warden.
  4. In the top right, click the Add Warden button to add a warden to the hostel.
  5. Select the employee department.
  6. Select the employee.
  7. Click the Save button.
