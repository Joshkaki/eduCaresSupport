How hostel fee can be collected from finance module?

The Administrator or a Privileged Employee (Fee Submission privilege Revert Transaction privileges required) can collect hostel fees from the Finance module. Though you will be able to create a partial fee payment transaction for finance fees, for hostel fees, you must create a full payment_transaction.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Collect the Fees > Fee Submission by batch.
  2. Select a batch and the hostel fee collection.
  3. Process the fee payment.

Or

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Finance > Fees > Collect the Fees > Fee submission for each student.
  2. In the Search field, enter the student name or admission number to search.

  4. Click Pay All Fees.
  5. Process the fee payment.
