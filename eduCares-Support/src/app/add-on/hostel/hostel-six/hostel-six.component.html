How to add hostels?

Hostels can be of three types:

  • Gents– Can be allocated to only male students.
  • Ladies – Can be allocated to only female students.
  • Common – Can be allocated to both male and female students.
  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Administration > Hostel > Hostel to open_the Hostel Details page.
  2. In the top right, click the Add hostel button to open the Add hostel page.
  3. In the Hostel Name field, enter the name of the hostel.
  4. In the Hostel Type field, select the hostel type – Gents, Ladies, or Common.
  5. In the Other Information field, enter any additional information about the hostel.
  6. Click the Save button.
