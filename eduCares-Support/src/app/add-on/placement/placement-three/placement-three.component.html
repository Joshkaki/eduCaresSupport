How to invite students for a placement?

In EduCares admin or privileged employee can create placement.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics> Placement
  2. In the table displays, click on appropriate placement title.
  3. Click on Invite students at the right top.
  4. Select batch and recipient to invite students to event.
