How can I edit a placement?

In EduCares admin or privileged employee can edit placement.

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Academics> Placement
  2. Click on Edit under actions for the respective placement event.
  3. Make necessary changes and click on Update placement event.

Note: Archived placement event cannot be edited
