Is it possible to edit a custom report? How can I delete a custom report?

An existing custom student report/ employee report cannot be edited. If any change needs to be made to the report, delete the report and create a new report. To delete a custom report:

  1. At the top of your dashboard, click the module access icon > Data and Reports > Custom Reports.
  2. Click Delete to delete the report.
